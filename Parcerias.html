From 26th August to 26th October 2014, FGV\EMAp will celebrate FGV 70th anniversary by hosting the Exhibit:

"Reshaping History visits Rio de Janeiro"

The "Reshaping History: A Future for our Past" exhibition aims to increase professional awareness of the growing use of 3D technology in documenting and interpreting cultural heritage. It illustrates how 3D technology can be used in scanning and modelling artefacts, archaeological sites and monuments discussing and illustrating the value of 3D in conservation and reconstruction.
