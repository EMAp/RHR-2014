September 1st:

"Workshop of 3D Technologies for Cultural Heritage Applications"

Aligned with the opening of the "Reshaping History" exposition, an open workshop will be held to illustrate how 3D technology may collaborate with the fields related to cultural heritage. All talks are open to the public, and will take place in the main auditorium at FGV. Registration for the workshop is free. A simultaneous interpretation service will be available.
